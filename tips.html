Student Travel Tips

  1. Flights: Track prices early and use alerts; consider nearby airports.
  2. Lodging: Compare hostels, dorms, and short-term rentals—check transit time to your activities.
  3. Food: Aim for one “treat” meal per trip; save with street food and grocery-store snacks.
  4. Budget: Set a daily cap; keep a simple note of spending in your phone.
  5. Safety: Share your itinerary, carry a portable battery, and know local emergency numbers.
