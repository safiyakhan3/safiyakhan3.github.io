Recurring Themes

I started noticing repeated motifs across trips. Naming these patterns helps me focus photos and activities.

  • Water: lakes, rivers, harbors
  • Nature: parks, trails, scenic overlooks
  • Monuments: public art, memorials, landmarks
  • Stores: markets, bookshops, local makers
  • People: street scenes and community spaces
